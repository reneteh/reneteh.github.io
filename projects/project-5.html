Evian, Journey to the Spring of Youth

Activation Advertising, Campaign Storytelling — 2025

This project began with an exploration of Evian’s brand identity, particularly its long-standing association with purity, youth, and natural origin.

Rather than treating these ideas as surface-level branding cues, the process focused on uncovering how Evian constructs the perception of “youth” through narrative and visual language. This led to the concept of The Mountain of Youth, a speculative extension of the brand that imagines the source of Evian water not just as a location, but as a symbolic space tied to rejuvenation, mythology, and transformation.

Through research and iterative ideation, the project developed from abstract brand analysis into a more immersive and experiential direction. Early explorations tested different ways of visualising the mountain, ranging from graphic interpretations to spatial storytelling, before refining it into a cohesive visual system. The process involved experimenting with composition, form, and scale to create a sense of elevation and discovery, while maintaining alignment with Evian’s clean and minimal aesthetic. This stage was crucial in balancing conceptual storytelling with brand consistency, ensuring that the outcome felt both imaginative and believable.

The final outcome presents The Mountain of Youth as a visual and narrative extension of Evian’s identity, transforming the idea of its source into an aspirational experience. Rather than simply advertising the product, the project builds a world around it, inviting audiences to engage with the notion of youth as something to be sought, experienced, and imagined. By bridging branding with speculative storytelling, the project repositions Evian not just as bottled water, but as a symbol of renewal, elevating its identity beyond function into a more emotive and experiential space.
